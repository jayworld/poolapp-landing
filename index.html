Everything you need to know about PoolPulse

What test kits does PoolPulse work with? +

PoolPulse works with any standard pool test kit - whether it's test strips, liquid drop kits, or digital testers. As long as you can measure your chlorine, pH, alkalinity, and stabiliser levels, you're good to go. We support all the major brands you'd find at Bunnings or your local pool shop.

Is this for saltwater or chlorine pools? +

Both! PoolPulse is built specifically for Aussie conditions, which means it handles salt chlorinator systems perfectly. Whether you've got a traditional chlorine pool or a saltwater system, the app gives you the right treatment plan for your setup.

What if my pool has a unique problem? +

PoolPulse is built on the Trouble Free Pool (TFP) methodology - the gold standard for pool chemistry. It handles the most common issues Aussie pool owners face: green water, cloudy water, high phosphates, algae, and chemical imbalances. If you've got a truly unusual situation, the app will guide you through the basics first, which solves 95% of problems.

Absolutely not. That's the whole point! You just punch in your test readings, and PoolPulse does all the calculations for you. It tells you exactly what to add, how much, and in what order. No formulas, no guesswork, no forum deep-dives at 11pm.
